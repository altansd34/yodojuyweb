I Dont Want My Daughter to Do That, Kylie Jenner Reveals Her Plastic Surgery Regrets

August 27, 2024
Celeb

“I wish I never touched anything to begin with,” that’s what 25-year-old Kylie Jenner said about her appearance. In the latest trailer of The Kardashians Season 3, she opened up about her regrets concerning plastic surgery. The model is worried about the influence it might have on her daughter and children around the globe.

Her crush said her lips were “small.”

Dee Cercone/Everett Collection/East News, Evan Agostini/Invision/East News

Kylie said she felt insecure after someone she had a crush on made fun of her lips. She was only 15 at the time, “It was one of my first kisses, and a guy was like, ’I didn’t think you’d be a good kisser because you have such small lips.’” The star said it had really hit her hard since it was coming from a guy she liked. She said, “I didn’t feel desirable or pretty.”

She always tried to make her lips look bigger.

Kylie admits, “I really wanted bigger lips. I would overline my lips with a lip liner just to create the illusion of bigger lips. And then finally I was like ’this lip liner isn’t doing it,’ and I ended up getting my lips done.”

She did that without her mother’s consent. Then she turned 18 years old, and her face shape started to transform because of the injections, which makes some surgeons believe she had a nose job as well.

Her mother got upset about what Kylie had done.

Kris Jenner revealed that she hadn’t actually given her daughter Kylie the permission to get lip fillers at 17. Kris said she went “crazy” when she found out what Kylie had done.

Kris says, “I have definite thoughts and ideas on what she should do at a really young age. In my day, some of this stuff didn’t exist, I don’t think I did anything physically to myself until my 50s. So I’m thinking why do you need to do that?”

Kylie admits she regrets her decisions.

The model opened up about her plastic surgery regrets during a heart-to-heart talk with her best friend Anastasia “Stassie” Karanikolaou. The 25-year-old said she wished she never went under the knife and wants her daughter to avoid it altogether, “I don’t want my daughter to do the things that I did. I wish I’d never touched anything to begin with,” says the model.

She wants to make a difference.

Kylie is aware of the influence their family has on the world, and she’s trying to change it. She’s worried about the negative impact the imposed standards have on children. The star claims, “All of us just need to have a bigger conversation about the beauty standards that we’re setting.”

In the latest The Kardashians trailer, she also mentioned, “We have huge influence. What are we doing with our power?”
