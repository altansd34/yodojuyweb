In an electrifying turn of events, the highly anticipated second season of ABC’s hit reality competition series, Claim to Fame, shines the spotlight on the dynamic duo of Kevin and Frankie Jonas. As the oldest and youngest members of the renowned Jonas brothers, Kevin and Frankie step into the roles of hosts, infusing the show with their infectious energy, personal experiences, and an undeniable chemistry that captivates viewers.

Claim To Fame Gets a New Family Dynamic With Kevin, Frankie Jonas

The second season of Claim to Fame promises to entertain viewers with its innovative blend of celebrity lineage, personal exploration, and the intricate tapestry of family dynamics. The show’s premise remains unchanged: a dozen contestants, all related to prominent celebrities, vie for a coveted $100,000 prize by concealing their famous lineage.

However, what sets this season apart is the exploration of the intricate web of family dynamics and the delicate balance between fame and personal identity. Kevin, aged 35, opens up during the sneak peek, offering a glimpse into the magnitude of the talent hidden within the competitors’ family trees.

“The 12 people we’ve invited into this house are not famous themselves, but they are all related to someone who is,” Kevin said.

Meanwhile, Frankie, affectionately known as the “Bonus Jonas,” brings a fresh and youthful perspective to the show. Having grown up alongside his internationally famous siblings, Nick and Joe, Frankie understands the complexities and challenges that come with being part of an A-list family.

Last season, Frankie shared his intrigue and captivation with the show’s central theme of identity. He marveled at the intricate dance between lies, deceit, and the exhilarating challenges that unfolded before their eyes.

Kevin, fully in sync with his younger brother’s sentiments, reveals that both he and Frankie immersed themselves in the experience, embracing their roles as hosts with gusto. Initially driven by a desire to uncover the truth, they soon realized that playing along with the contestants brought an element of excitement and fun to their hosting duties.

What’s New On Claim To Fame?

The contestants will be living in a new house that is equipped with hidden cameras and microphones. This means that the contestants will be under constant surveillance, and they’ll have to be careful not to let their secrets slip.

The show will also feature new twists in the many Claim To Fame challenges. Season Two of Claim To Fame is sure to be full of drama. The contestants are all vying for the same prize, and they’re not afraid to lie, cheat, and steal to get ahead.

This season is sure to be full of twists and turns, and it’s sure to keep you guessing until the very end. Claim To Fame Season Two is currently airing on ABC. New episodes air weekly on Mondays at 8 p.m. ET. You can also watch the episodes on Hulu the next day.
