Dancing With The Stars standout JoJo Siwa recently updated fans about what she’ll be up to in 2023. She also revealed some changes in her inbox, apparently removing close friend Jenna Johnson in her pinned conversations.

Earlier in 2022, JoJo Siwa created a TikTok video giving a glimpse of her current interests and personal priorities. The DWTS standout dared herself to duet the same video by the end of the year to see what has changed since then.

Comparing the outset of 2023 from the beginning of her 2022, Siwa casually revealed who were added and removed in her pinned contacts. Apparently, her DWTS partner Jenna Johnson, who was pinned in Siwa’s inbox before, wasn’t pinned anymore. This gives rise to the possibility that the two might not be as in touch as before.

Back in July 2022, Siwa described Johnson as her “family” and “bestfriend,” being one of the most excited people with her pregnancy. Furthermore, it’s no secret that Siwa had an eventful 2021 with her impressive run in DWTS season 30 and painful split with Kylie Prew. According to her, Johnson guided her and helped her find happiness with herself during those times.

Be that as it may, Siwa and Johnson are most likely just occupied with other personal matters. With Johnson’s baby nearly coming and Siwa dealing with her own projects, the two most probably don’t have time to catch up.

She Wants to Focus on Music, Strength Training This Year

Siwa’s random obsessions have also changed over the course of a year. Some of these include jeans, learning the drums, and listening to Miley Cyrus and Fletcher. In the same TikTok video, Siwa has also reflected on the entire year of 2022.

“What a year. Lots of ups and lots of downs, but I’m very grateful for EVERYTHING and every experience I had in 2022!! Here we go 2023!!” she wrote.

Not mentioning about the specifics, Siwa also announced that she is prioritizing her music career this year. Such goal shall hopefully be done alongside her physical health aims like muscle strength training.
