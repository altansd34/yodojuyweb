America’s Got Talent Season 18 contestants Eseniia Mikheeva and Avantgardey recently teamed up to perform an adorable dance routine, which they shared on social media. The dancers will all be appearing on this week’s live show.

AGT‘s Eseniia Mikheeva, Avantgardey Perform Together

Seven-year-old dancer Eseniia Mikheeva charmed the judges and then blew them away with her fierce dance moves during her audition for Season 18 of AGT. Eseniia was born in Russia but now lives with her family in the United States.

Avantgardey is an all-female dance group from Japan. The group members all wear matching clothes and the same bob haircut. Their synchronized moves impressed the judges during their audition earlier this season.

On Sunday, Eseniia and Avantgardey collaborated on a cute dance routine set to “Beggin'” by Måneskin. Eseniia starts the video (posted to TikTok and Instagram) with her normal hair, and then she ends up with a bob to match Avantgardey.

Both Eseniia and Avantgardey will be competing in this week’s AGT live show for a chance to go to the Finals of Season 18. Only two acts from Tuesday’s show will move on from the overnight vote.

It’s the Last Week Before the Finals

This week marks the last Season 18 live show before the Finals. The competition was narrowed down to 55 acts after the auditions. Each week, 11 acts have taken the stage, with two acts from each group making it to the finale thanks to America’s vote.

This week’s show will be similar, but there’s a twist. Host Terry Crews teased last week that this Wednesday’s results show will also feature a wildcard vote. The judges will each choose an eliminated act that they think deserves a spot in the finale.

Fans will vote for their favorite of the chosen acts during the results show. The act with the most votes will become a wildcard act, meaning there will be a total of 11 acts in the Finals this year.
