Brother-sister roller skating duo Billy and Emily England will take the America’s Got Talent: The Champions stage in Monday’s new episode. Lucky for us, the show shared a sneak peek of the pair’s performance on their YouTube page. It’s just as wild as we’ve come to expect.

Billy and Emily made it to the semifinals in AGT Season 12, and were previously contestants on Britain’s Got Talent. They now perform in Las Vegas. If you’re unfamiliar with their act, we’ll warn you that this is definitely not your mama’s roller skating. They do some pretty dangerous things on those wheels, and we’re not just talking about forgoing helmets.

Add in the stunt where Billy balances Emily on his head, and there’s not a whole lot of new material here. However, the shocked faces on the judges and audience as they do their thing are proof that the wow factor certainly hasn’t worn off. Judge Simon Cowell even gives them an immediate standing ovation at the end of the clip. Could this be his Golden Buzzer act for the season?

If these two make it through, it will be interesting to see how they fare against fellow danger duo Deadly Games. Does America prefer knife throwing or extreme Xanadu? I suppose we’ll just have to wait and see.
