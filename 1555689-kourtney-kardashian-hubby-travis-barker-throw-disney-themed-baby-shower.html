Kourtney Kardashian, Hubby Travis Barker Throw Disney-Themed Baby Shower

August 24, 2024

  • The mum of three and her hubby threw a Disney-themed party for their baby shower

Reality star Kourtney Kardashian has thrown a Disney-themed baby shower.

Trip to Disneyland in Kourtney's backyard

Over the weekend, the couple threw a Disney-themed celebration featuring delightful Mickey Mouse-shaped snacks.

There was an embroidery station for customising iconic Mickey Mouse ears hats from Disneyland, a whimsical entrance sign proclaiming 'Baby Barker.'

A Snow White-inspired apple tree and even a lively performance by Main Street's Dapper Dans (a quartet that entertains at Disney).

According to E-Online, Kourtney, Travis and their guests, including sister Khloé Kardashian and Adrienne Bailon, shared glimpses of the enchanting event on their Instagram stories.

What Kourtney and Barker wore

For this special occasion, the mum of three made a stunning impression in a snug-fitting jumpsuit adorned with a distinctive brown-and-white snakeskin design.

To complete her look, the Poosh CEO donned stylish black platform heels and sported a pair of sleek, narrow black sunglasses.

Barker's outfit was effortlessly cool, featuring a black t-shirt, blue jeans, a skeleton print bomber jacket, black shoes, and sleek black sunglasses.

They both added a personal touch by wearing customised Mickey Mouse ears hats.

Kim Kardashian and her daughter, North, also wore matching neon outfits.

Kourtney announces she's expecting

In June, the reality TV star and Barker announced to the world that they were expecting their first child together.

While most celebrities are fond of announcing their pregnancy reveals through beautiful photos, Kourtney chose to use a concert to share the good news.
