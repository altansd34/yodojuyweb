Former Nickelodeon star Drake Bell was recently unmasked on Mexico’s version of The Masked Singer. Bell competed in the costume Baby Alien, which was previously featured in the fourth season of the American series.

Drake Bell Unmasked on The Masked Singer in Mexico

The Mexican version of The Masked Singer is titled ¿Quién es la Máscara? Earlier this week, Bell performed on the show as Bebé Alien. In Season 4 of the U.S. show, former NFL star Mark Sanchez performed in a similar costume.

Bell was eliminated and unmasked. In a video from his unmasking, Bell told one of the panelists, “I thought you would get it!” He also sang a few lines from “Found a Way,” which is the theme song to his former Nickelodeon show Drake & Josh.

“Thank you very much @cuquis_rg and @foxmuller29 for inviting me to participate in this show!” Bell wrote on Instagram in Spanish, tagging producers on the show. “This was so much fun! Thank you very much for your support!!!”

Bell Was Put on Probation in 2021

Bell hasn’t been a stranger to controversy in recent years. In 2021, he pleaded guilty over a case of attempted child endangerment and was sentenced to two years probation. Bell was also arrested for DUI in 2015 and was accused of abuse by his ex-girlfriend in 2020. He filed for bankruptcy in 2014.

Earlier this year, Bell was reported missing and considered by the police to be “endangered,” but he was later found safe. Bell laughed off the incident on social media, writing, “You leave your phone in the car and don’t answer for the night and this?” However, reports suggest Bell might have been suicidal.

Not long after this incident, Bell’s wife Janet Von Schmeling filed for divorce after four years of marriage. The couple shares a son, Jeremy Drake Bell.
