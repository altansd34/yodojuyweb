She currently lives in Lexington, Kentucky
Givens played a nurse on an episode of Good Times, "The Evans Get Involved, Part 2," in 1977. Givens was the 1989 Grand Prize Winner of the Crown Royal Comedy Contest held at the The Regal Theater in Chicago, Illinois.
