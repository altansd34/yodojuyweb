What are the names of My Little Pony characters?

If you are curious about the original My Little Pony names, there are six primary characters who are widely known as the "Mane Six". They defend their magical country, Equestria, from various threats. Here is My Little Pony names list for six most famous protagonists.

  • Princess Twilight Sparkle. She is the central personality of the series. This character has a purple colour scheme with a dark-blue and pink mane. She is known for her intellect, as well as her love for science and adventures. She is a natural leader who often resolves conflicts between her friends.
  • Applejack. She is orange and has blonde hair. Her main characteristic is "farm girl," as she works as an apple farmer and speaks with a southern accent. She represents honesty and courage and is very down-to-earth compared to her friends.
  • Rainbow Dash. This little horse has a blue colouring and a rainbow mane, which explains her name choice. She is obsessed with adventures and is very athletic, with a goal to join the Wonderbolts aerobatic team. She is a representation of loyalty.
  • Pinkie Pie. This character is pink with a magenta mane. She is the comedian in the series, as she is involved in multiple funny scenes. She often throws parties and makes her friends feel less stressed, but also lacks confidence and has a great fear of rejection. Pinkie Pie represents laughter.
  • Rarity. She is a white unicorn who has a violet mane, Rarity has one of the most beautiful My Little Pony unicorn names. Being a huge fashionista, she is often melodramatic and may seem arrogant. However, she has a kind heart and represents generosity. She sees the inner beauty in everyone.
  • Fluttershy. This pegasus is yellow in colour and has a pink mane. She loves nature and animals, which she can communicate with. She is shy, sweet, and symbolises kindness.

My Little Pony names and cutie marks

All the main characters have a "cutie mark," which is a unique symbol that indicates their special talent. You can find My Little Pony names and symbols below, and discover which special talent each magical creature has.

  • Twilight Sparkle's mark is a six-pointed star, which is surrounded by five more small stars. She got this one because she was a magic student and eventually improved her magical abilities.
  • Rainbow Dash has a cloud mark with a rainbow-coloured lightning bolt. The meaning of this symbol originates from her love for speed and showing off her athletic moves.
  • Pinkie Pie's cutie mark is three balloons. Those balloons symbolise her finding enjoyment in throwing parties, as well as her social activity and energetic personality.
  • Rarity has three diamonds as her cutie mark, which suggests that she is as rare as a diamond.
  • Applejack's marking is three red apples, which is a symbol of her life at Sweet Apple Acres and the joy she finds in farming.
  • Fluttershy has three butterflies, which are a representation of her passion for taking care of animals and communicating with them.
  • Princess Celestia is marked with an eight-pointed sun, which indicates that she is responsible for raising the sun.
