She played Ariel in a Disney live-action puppet show called Little Mermaid's Island. DePrima and her husband, actor George Newbern, both graduated from Northwestern University, where she was a member of Kappa Alpha Theta,[2] in 1986 with degrees in Radio/TV/Film.
