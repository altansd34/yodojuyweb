On Monday night, NBC aired the first night of the America’s Got Talent: Fantasy League Semifinals. Two acts were sent to the Finals thanks to Golden Buzzers from judges Howie Mandel and Mel B, while a third act was voted through by the audience.

Who Earned Golden Buzzers in the AGT: Fantasy League Semifinals?

For the first night of the Semifinals, 10 acts took the stage. Judges Mel B and Howie Mandel were tasked with hitting the Golden Buzzer for acts they thought deserved a spot in the Finals. First, Mel hit the Golden Buzzer for aerialist Aidan Bryant.

Aidan, who came in second place in AGT Season 16 and went on to win AGT: All-Stars, had been competing on Heidi Klum’s team. For the Semifinals, he returned with a gravity-defying routine that had the judges screaming. It was enough to get Mel B to hit the Golden Buzzer and steal Aidan for her team.

Later in the show, AGT Season 14 winner Kodi Lee took the stage on Simon Cowell’s team to perform his original song “Journey of You and I,” dedicated to his mom Tina. Judge Howie Mandel was so impressed, his hit the Golden Buzzer to steal Kodi from Cowell’s team and send him to the Finals.

Pack Drumline Was Voted Through to the Finale

At the end of the night, the audience voters got to decide on one more act who should move on to the Fantasy League Finals. Ultimately, that spot went to The Pack Drumline, competing on Simon Cowell’s team.

The group delivered an electrifying performance of “Eye of The Tiger” by Survivor and “Panda” by Desiigner. It included some impressive acrobatics and a creative use of props. Afterward, Cowell told them, “I could jump on this desk with excitement.”

Next week will mark the second night of the Semifinals, determining the last group of acts in the finale. Those acts will join past Golden Buzzer acts Billy and Emily England, the Ramadhani Brothers, Sofie Dossi, and Sainted in competing for the grand prize.
