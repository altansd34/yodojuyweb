The upcoming season of Dancing With The Stars has a new family-themed approach, and it starts with the new co-hosts Julianne Hough and Alfonso Ribeiro. In a recent interview, Hough and Ribeiro expressed their excitement about co-hosting together and their goal of making the show feel like a warm and welcoming family again.

Julianne Hough, Alfonso Ribeiro are Set to Co-Host DWTS

Season 32 of DWTS promises to be a fun-filled, family-friendly affair, with the returning judges, Derek Hough, Carrie Ann Inaba, and Bruno Tonioli, all looking forward to reuniting with the show’s loyal fans. In season 32, DWTS is once again placing emphasis on those values, with co-hosts Hough and Ribeiro determined to make it a season to remember.

“We wanna make it family again. Make it really just warm and everybody have a great time,” Ribeiro said.

“Honestly, it’s just family again. I’m just excited. I can’t wait to see everybody back on the dance floor and just get our groove on,” Hough said.

While the show has undergone some changes over the years, it has always remained true to its core values of family, fun, and entertainment.

With a renewed focus on family and a great lineup of hosts and judges, DWTS season 32 is sure to be a hit. Viewers can expect to see amazing dance performances, great chemistry between the hosts and judges, and a warm and welcoming atmosphere that feels like a big family reunion.

Which Pros Are Returning for DWTS Season 32?

Fans of the popular dance competition show are eagerly awaiting the announcement of which professional dancers will be returning for the new season. The show typically announces the pro cast ahead of the celebrity cast in late summer. While there’s no official word yet, some rumors have been circulating about who might be returning.

According to various sources, the following pros are expected to return for Season 32: Alan Bersten, Artem Chigvintsev, Brandon Armstrong, Britt Stewart, Gleb Savchenko, Jenna Johnson Chmerkovskiy, Keo Motsepe, Pasha Pashkov, Sasha Farber, Sharna Burgess, and Val Chmerkovskiy. Some of these pros have taken breaks in recent seasons, but are reportedly eager to return to the show.

There are also some pros who may not be returning due to personal reasons. Cheryl Burke and Mark Ballas have retired from the show after Season 31, while several others are taking time off to start families. Sharna Burgess, Lindsay Arnold, Witney Carson, and Daniella Karagach will have young children when Season 32 comes around.
