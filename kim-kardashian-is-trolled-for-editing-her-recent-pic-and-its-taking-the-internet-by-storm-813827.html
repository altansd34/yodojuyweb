Kim Kardashian Is Trolled for Editing Her Recent Pic, and Its Taking the Internet by Storm

August 28, 2024
Celeb

Kim Kardashian’s recent pic has taken the internet by storm. The socialite’s Instagram photo was deemed to be edited after the photographer’s original version has surfaced online. We’d like you to compare both and decide what you think of the difference between the two snaps.

She’s the Queen of social media.

The 42-year-old shared with her over 360 million Instagram followers a series of snaps from the Louis Vuitton Fashion Show which she recently attended.

The post quickly became a huge hit as fans showered Kim with praise and positive comments about her strikingly good looks and gorgeous figure.

Can you spot the differences?

Things took an unexpected turn after an Instagram account named ProblematicFame made a comparison between Kim’s photo and the photographer’s original image.

The comparison showed both pics together and claimed that the reality star edited part of her body and face such as her ’’stomach, waist, arms, armpits, breasts, shoulders, jaw, chin, nasolabial folds, nose, lips, eyes, and skin texture.’’ The post noted, ’’It is very easy to find the original and compare.’’ That said, the account also acknowledged how stunning Kim is, saying that she ’’looks gorgeous without the photoshop.’’

Fans were left divided.

Although the claimed edits were in fact very tiny, online commentators were quick to share their thoughts with full honesty. One person noted, ’’Completely random and unnecessary edits.’’ While another wrote, ’’This is truly sad...her body is amazing as it is!’’

Others admitted that they were unable to differentiate the photoshopped version from the original photo. We think that this star is gorgeous either way.
