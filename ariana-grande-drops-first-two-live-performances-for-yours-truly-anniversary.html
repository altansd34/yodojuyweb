Ariana Grande has officially shared the first two live performances celebrating the 10th anniversary of her debut album Yours Truly. The singer recorded performances of “Honeymoon Avenue” and “Daydreamin'” to share with fans.

Ariana Grande Drops Two New Live Performances

The new performances were recorded in London, where Grande has recently been filming the upcoming Wicked movie adaptations. The singer wears a blue and black two-piece outfit with a full skirt. She performs the songs with a band and a string ensemble.

“Honeymoon Avenue” is the first track on the 2013 album. This new performance shows off how much Grande’s voice has matured over the past decade. Grande also released a video of herself performing “Daydreamin’,” surrounded by lights from a mirrorball.

On Instagram, Grande wrote that Yours Truly is “a project that changed my life.” She thanked fans “for listening and for growing with me every step of the way.”

These aren’t the only live performances Grande will be dropping as part of her 10th anniversary celebration. “Baby I” will be released Sunday, “Tattoed Heart” and “Right There” will be released Tuesday, and “The Way” will be released Wednesday.

Grande Released a Deluxe Version of the Album

As part of her 10th anniversary celebration, Grande released a deluxe digital version of Yours Truly on Friday. This release features new versions of several songs, including a Spanglish version of the lead single “The Way.”

The album also features the live versions of the six songs Grande performed in London. The cover of the 10th anniversary project features an image of Grande that appears to be from the shoot for her music video for “The Way.”
