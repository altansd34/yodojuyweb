18 Celebs Whove Had Plastic Surgery and Dont Try to Keep It a Secret

August 17, 2024
Celeb

As a rule, celebrities don’t talk about their visits to plastic surgeons and claim that their beauty is the result of a healthy lifestyle. But there are brave women who don’t want to hide the fact that in order to stay young, they have to turn to surgeons and undergo complicated procedures.

18. Britney Spears

EVERETT COLLECTION / EAST NEWS, AFP / EAST NEWS

Britney doesn’t keep it a secret that she has her “own” surgeon. But she’s only tried lip fillers as of now. Spears says it was “fun stuff.”

17. Chrissy Teigen

LCLA / Broadimage/East News, Ivan Nikolov / WENN.com / agefotostock / East News

The model admitted that everything about her is fake except her cheeks. Later, it turned out that she was kidding and the only surgery she’d had was armpit lipo.

16. Victoria Beckham

The singer had a breast augmentation when she was a member of the Spice Girls. But she regrets her decision now and doesn’t recommend that young girls change their bodies just because it’s fashionable. Today, Victoria is comfortable with her natural size.

15. Tyra Banks

EAST NEWS, Invision / Invision / East News

The model published a book where she revealed that she had a nose job early in her career. By the way, she often wears wigs and doesn’t keep that a secret.

14. Jennifer Grey

PARAMOUNT PICTURES / Album / EAST NEWS, EVERETT COLLECTION / EAST NEWS

The star of Dirty Dancing regrets getting a nose job. Her new nose stopped her from being popular after the film and she never managed to get any other successful roles.

13. Khloé Kardashian

In her interview, Khloé Kardashian says she’s had face fillers. But she didn’t like the results and got rid of them. As for her nose, Kardashian claims it’s natural.

12. Kaley Cuoco

East News, Jordan Strauss / Invision / AP / East News

The actress admits to having had breast implants and a nose job. She claims that these changes made her feel confident.

11. Gwyneth Paltrow

The actress admits that she’s tried everything except surgery. Contouring with Botox scared Gwyneth, because, in her words: “Botox made me look crazy.” But she isn’t opposed to plastic surgery and says that one day she’ll probably have to go under the knife.

10. Iggy Azalea

Matt Sayles / Invision / CAPM161 / East News, CHRIS DELMAS / AFP / East News

The hip-hop singer has had a breast augmentation and doesn’t keep it a secret. Her nose and chin have also changed and she says, “I’m not denying it.”

9. Jennifer Aniston

EAST NEWS, Paul Marks / Broadimage / EAST NEWS

If we compare Jennifer’s old and recent pictures, we’ll see that her nose has changed. The actress says she’s had septum surgery.

8. Amanda Bynes

Bynes has also had a nose job. She hates her old photos where she’s depicted with her old look.

7. Robin Wright

face to face / REPORTER / East News, Sipa USA / Sipa USA / East News

Robin Wright revealed that she visits a plastic surgeon regularly. Twice a year, Robin gets Botox and fillers. Today, she looks younger than she did 10 years ago.

6. Christie Brinkley

The 65-year-old model uses fillers, gets Xeomin injections, and whitens her skin. And to lift and tighten the skin, she uses laser and ultrasound devices (that she promotes).

5. Denise Richards

EAST NEWS, Janet Gough / AFF-USA.com / MEGA / Mega Agency / East News

The actress had her first surgery at the age of 19: she had her breasts enlarged. Then she made them even bigger. Today, Denise regrets her decision. In one of her interviews, she says she’s spent a lot of effort correcting all the consequences.

4. Cher

EAST NEWS, Joseph Gotfriedy / Broadimage / EAST NEWS

Cher doesn’t try to hide the fact that she’s had surgeries. She’s had a circular facelift, a rhinoplasty, and a breast augmentation. She says, “Yes, I’ve had a facelift, but who hasn’t?”

3. Courteney Cox

Gilbert Flores / Broadimage / EAST NEWS, Invision / Invision / East News

The former Friends star has had some major issues with her plastic surgery. Fillers and Botox changed her beyond recognition. Courteney regretted it and decided to not have any plastic surgery anymore.

2. Nicole Kidman

AFPEAST NEWS, JEAN-BAPTISTE LACROIX / AFP / East News

Mass media often blames Nicole for having lots of surgeries: from a breast augmentation to fake teeth. But the actress admits that she has only gotten facial contouring with Botox.

1. Dana Delany

EVERETT COLLECTION / EAST NEWS, ADMEDIA / SIPA / EAST NEWS

The former Desperate Housewives actress says she’s had a Botox injection once. But she regrets it since the procedure made her face look asymmetrical.
