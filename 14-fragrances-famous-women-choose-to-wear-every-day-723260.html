14Fragrances Famous Women Choose toWear Every Day

August 17, 2024
Celeb

Along with models, more and more famous actresses and singers are becoming the faces of perfume brands. And then the reasonable question arises: what fragrances do these celebrities really choose to wear in their everyday life?

Bright Side peeked into the beauty bags of famous women to find out what perfume we should buy to smell more like Victoria Beckham, Cindy Crawford, and other celebrities.

Beyoncé

In her interviews, Beyoncé admits that she loves everything that is created under the Giorgio Armani brand, including perfumes. Emporio Armani Diamonds Intense is her all-time favorite fragrance which, she believes, perfectly accentuates her femininity.

Jennifer Aniston

Jennifer Aniston decided not to choose just one smell from a great variety of perfumes and created a whole line under her own brand. Chapter One is her favorite fragrance. Its bottle looks like a teardrop, while its composition is a mix of florals, citrus, and woody notes.

Kim Kardashian

The reality show star decided to mix business with pleasure and created a perfume she’d like to wear herself and managed to make some money out of the idea as well. Kim Kardashian has been producing her own perfume line for women for several years. Among all the fragrances, she has 3 favorites: the recently released perfumes in heart-shaped multi-colored bottles called Wifey, Baddi, and Baby Girl.

Naomi Watts

The British actress shared in an interview that she doesn’t like overpowering perfumes on other people or herself. Musk by American brand Kiehl’s has become the perfect fragrance for her since it’s fresh and can be compared with the just-got-out-of-the-shower smell.

Kate Bosworth

The American actress and model admits that she has been a fan of a Swedish brand Byredo and has been loyal to its perfume called Gypsy Water for many years. The manufacturer describes this fragrance in quite a romantic way as it has the scent of soil, deep forests, and campfires.

Sarah Jessica Parker

Sarah Jessica Parker has already created many perfume lines for both men and women. In one of the interviews, the actress revealed that she took inspiration from her favorite perfume, Incense Avignon by a Japanese brand Comme des Garcons to create her latest line of unisex fragrances called Stash.

Cindy Crawford

The former supermodel doesn’t stint when it comes to choosing a perfume and orders it from a celebrity perfumer Azzi Glasser. The estimated cost of the original composition is 15,000 GBR. Azzi Glasser says that she creates unique aromas that reveal the soul and the character of people. Among her regular clients are such celebrities as Orlando Bloom, Johnny Depp, Jude Law, Helena Bonham Carter, and Kate Moss.

Kylie Jenner

Kylie Jenner admits that she doesn’t like heavy perfumes that can cause headaches. The model chooses perfumed body sprays. Her favorite fragrance is Coconut Passion by Victoria’s Secret.

Gigi Hadid

The American supermodel was a part of an advertising campaign for Velvet Orchid perfume by Tom Ford. Hadid admits that she uses this perfume all the time. And if she has to attend an evening event, she always chooses Velvet Orchid.

Kendall Jenner

The model Kendall Jenner chooses Modern Muse Le Rouge fragrance by Estee Lauder in her everyday life. The manufacturer of the perfume claims that it’s dedicated to a modern woman — a brave and alluring muse who wants to impress people around her.

Victoria Beckham

Victoria Beckham is in love with the Room Service fragrance by a selective brand Vilhelm Parfumerie. The quite unusual perfume name can be explained by the story of its creation. It’s dedicated to Hollywood star, Greta Garbo, who always tried to hide her private life from the public eye and hid from the outside world in her hotel room.

Katy Perry

Famous for her provocative style, the singer admits that she adores the Angel fragrance by Thierry Mugler. She believes this perfume stands out because it’s creative, luscious, and distinct.

Keira Knightley

For many years Keira Knightley has been playing a femme fatale in the commercials for the legendary fragrance Coco Mademoiselle by Chanel. And all these years, according to the actress, she associates this smell with herself and wears it in her everyday life.

Reese Witherspoon

The actress reveals that she never leaves the house without using her favorite Amber Musk by Aerin Lauder first. The manufacturer describes this perfume as “a warm, inviting smell you want to wrap yourself up in — like a soft cozy blanket on a cold snowy night.”

Do you prefer the same fragrances as any celebrities? Let us know in the comments which perfume is your favorite!
