America’s Got Talent Season 14 finalist Benicio Bryant has been teasing fans about an upcoming single and possible music video on social media this month. The track is called “I Can’t Dance to This,” and it’s set to be released June 30.

Benicio Bryant Teases New Song, Music Video

Earlier in June, Benicio posted an 18-second clip from his new song “I Can’t Dance to This” on Instagram, telling his fans to pre-save it. It sounds like a bouncy pop song, and contrary to the title, we predict we’ll be dancing along. The pre-save link lists June 30 as the release date.

Then a few days ago, the 17-year-old teased that he would have an “announcement soon.” On Thursday, he took to Instagram and TikTok to post a brief clip that looks like it was taken on the set of a music video.

Benicio appears on a monitor, wearing a bright green jacket and and staring into the camera. The singer captioned the Instagram post, “Ready…..???” It’s possible this is from a music video shoot to go along with the new song. Fans expressed their excitement in the comments.

What Has the Singer Been Up to Since AGT?

Benicio auditioned for Season 14 of America’s Got Talent in 2019, when he was just 14 years old. He wowed the judges with a cover of Brandi Carlile’s “The Joke” and went on to sing several original songs throughout the competition.

The singer reached the Top 10, although he didn’t win the show. He went on to sign with Arista Records and has released several singles over the past few years. They include “Ghosted,” “Sorry,” and recent release “WHO HURT YOU?”

Benicio has updated his style, opting for more colorful, glam looks. He also dyed his hair blond, although he seems to have gone back to brown recently. Benicio said in a TikTok video that he’s “happy” with how his style has evolved.
